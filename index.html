The Field of Medicine

Medicine is the practice of diagnosing and treating a patient of their injury or disease. It revolves around the prevention and treatment of diseases or injuries. There are many parts of science which are all considered to be a medical profession, as it varies. Some examples of medical professions are biomedical engineer, surgeon, physican, nurse, and much more. Furthermore, there are even more jobs within these professions, as there are scrub nurses, cardiothoraic surgeons, neurosurgeons, etc.
